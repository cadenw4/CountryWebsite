Wonton noodle soup is an iconic Cantonese comfort food, a symphony of textures and umami where plump, shrimp-filled wontons with tender skins meet springy, duck-egg "bamboo noodles" in a deeply savory broth, often simmered for hours from pork bones and dried seafood, garnished simply with chives and shrimp roe. Wontons are delicate parcels filled with a mix of shrimp and pork, aiming for a crunchy texture from fresh shrimp and a tender wrapper.
