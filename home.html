What Is Hong Kong Known For?

Hong Kong, known as the one of the global financial capitals of the world, is a gold mine for new economic opportunities. Hong Kong is known for their famous and unique architechure- holding the most amount of skyscrapers in the world! The Hong Kong skyline is unlike no other, with a vibrant and colorful nightlife and a peaceful and natural view during the day. Not only that, Hong Kong is also well known for their vast variety of food. It is famous for their cantonese styled dishes such as Char siu or Dim Sum.
