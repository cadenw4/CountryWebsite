Disney Land Hong Kong

Hong Kong Disney Land, or HKDL, is a park located in Penny’s Bay, Lantau Island, Hong Kong. Opening in 2005, the park now remains standing for 20+ years! It is the second Disney park in Asia, opening after Disneyland Tokyo in 1983. Hong Kong Disneyland is also known as the smallest Disneyland in the world! This park includes classic Disney attractions such as Its a Small World and Cinderella’s Carousel, while also offering a blend of Chinese culture and Disney magic with Disney inspired Dim Sum and distinct and exclusive rides such as Iron Man Experience & Ant-Man and The Wasp.
